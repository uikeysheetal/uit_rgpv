University Institute of Technology Rajiv Gandhi Technical University

ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY

University Institute of technology RGPV,popularly known as UIT RGTU,is a technical institute located in Bhopal,India. THe institute was established by the government of Madhya Pradesh with the name of "Government Engineering Collage"as an autonomous institute in the year 1986.AICTE (All India Council of Technical Education) Application No./UGC Registration No./College Code is 1-136924

History

To address the growing need of engineering expertise, the government of Madhya Pradesh created Government Engineering College (GEC) in 1986 in the state capital Bhopal. The institute initially offered Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) only in mechanical engineering. Courses in Mining Engineering and computer science and engineering were included when it was declared autonomous in 2002. At the same time it was made a part of Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyala (RGPV), the technological university of the state of Madhya Pradesh. The name of the institute was then changed to its current name (UIT RGPV).

Facilities in the University

Central Library

The library has the rich collection of all kinds of text and references books related to all the subjects of various disciplines.

College Hostel

The institute has three boys hostel and two girls hostels having a total of 300 seats and 320 seats respectively.

College Canteen

Newly constructed canteen exists adjoining to alumni transit home and opposite to VIP guest house.

Games & sports

The college provides excellent facilities for practically all major outdoor and indoor games and has a full time sports officer with adequate supporting staff.

Medical /Dispensary

A Dispensary providing students and staff with basic medical facilities has been set up in the campus with a qualified compounder.
